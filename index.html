Instructions

Here are the steps to get started:

  1. Learn: On the 'Learn' page, you can study and try to remember which flag belongs to which country.
  2. Guess the Country: This game shows you a flag and four country options. Try to recall which country the flag belongs to and select the correct answer.
  3. Guess the Flag: You'll see a country name and four flags. Remember which flag matches the country and click on it.

Each game consists of 12 questions. After answering all, you'll be directed to a results page where Dr. Sheldon Cooper from 'The Big Bang Theory' will rate your achievement with one of four possible scores based on how many you got right:

  • Less than 4 correct answers.
  • 4-7 correct answers.
  • 8-11 correct answers.
  • All 12 correct answers.

You can exit to the main page at any time by clicking the 'Home Page' link or restart the game by pressing the 'Restart the game' link.
